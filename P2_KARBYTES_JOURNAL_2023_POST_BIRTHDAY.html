19_APRIL_2023 Update: Although I wrote earlier this year on this web page that some of the journal chapters listed on this web page are “essential reading” (in terms of getting to know what my preferences, style, goals, and progress towards achieving those goals are), I now would like to comment that, if you want to read a single page of my website, you might as well read every page of my website to garner what that “essence of karbytes” is. I do not require that any information processing agent do that. Everything I publish on my personal websites and social media accounts is optional for any person to read. I do not wish to burden people with a sense of obligation to read my content given how scarce people’s cognitive resources are and how abundant subject matter to spend those cognitive resources on is. I prefer that each person be autonomous, healthy, productive, smart, and happy as possible and to pursue immortality, omniscience, and other so-called physically impossible states of existence (but I do not want to force my preferences on people other than myself).
